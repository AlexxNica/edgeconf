About Edge

Edge is a new kind of one-day conference on advanced web technologies for developers and browser vendors, presented by FT Labs and Google.

Tickets cost $100, which will pay panelists' travel expenses and help us ensure that the venue is full without having to oversell. Any money we have left will be donated to a local charity. For more information visit the conference website, http://edgeconf.com.
